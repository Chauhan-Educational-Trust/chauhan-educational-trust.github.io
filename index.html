About Us

Established in 2011, Seven Senses House of Children has now expanded to Seven Senses Whitefield and Seven Senses Indiranagar. Based on the Montessori philosophy of imparting education, our school is characteristic of mixed-age classrooms, freedom to choose work, special montessori and B.Ed.-trained teachers, and work environments containing a wide-array of materials. Our institution recognises that children learn in different ways, and therefore, the curriculum is catered to each individual child. In their earlier years, children work with materials on mats to follow lesson-plans and later transition to desks-and-chairs, integrating the traditional and modern clasroom environments to form a system that places emphasis on idependence, self-reliance, and self-motivation for work time. Our children compete at the international level in Olympiads to secure the first ranks world-over. Seven Senses has been awarded the title of "Most Promising school in Bangalore using Montesssori-teaching techniques" at the Education Excellence Awards conducted by ET Now. Our schools are one of the select-few to be recognised by the Indian Montessori Centre as well as the State Board of Karnataka. We follow the International IGCSE curriculum for higher classes.
