About Us

Seven Senses offers the learning environment based on the Montessori Method in Bangalore, Indiranagar. This comprehensive educational approach is child centric and is based on the observation of a child's needs in a variety of cultures all around the world. Our seven senses montessori school providing a high quality learning materials and infrastructure for a true Montessori experience. The environment offers a lot of natural light, is calming, wherein respect and courtesy to others is inculcated in every person at the school. A child is gently guided through the learning process by highly trained and courteous educators. A large variety of specially designed learning materials and activities are invitingly arranged on open shelves in specific and yet integrated areas of the environment - Practical Life, Sensorial, Language, Mathematics and Culture. Of course, Science, Music, Art and Physical Education are an important part of the daily curriculum too. Our objective is for children to become independent, self reliant, self aware, develop a sense of self worth, discover their unique identity, and build academic excellence in a natural manner.
