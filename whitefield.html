Our philosophy

Our teachings are based on the Montessori philosophy of education, and our objective is for children to become independent and driven. We nurture the uniqueness of each child by placing emphasis on "following the child" by creating an individual curriculum based on each child's needs.

What do we do?

Through the integration of varied age spans in environmets or classrooms, children work with sensorial, practical life, language mathematics, science and culture. The educators follow the development of each child, and give presentations depending on each indivdual child's pace.
